What is MCM Scholarship?!

Be A Catalyst And Create Change In The Lives Of Academically Brilliant Students With High Potential.

The objective of the Scheme is to provide financial assistance to the poor and meritorious students of tkmce to enable them to pursue professional and technical courses.

Things To Consider Before Applying

________________________________________________________

Academic Eligibility

  • Both PG and UG students are eligible to apply for this Scholarship.
  • Studnets can apply/reapply for this scholarship in each academic year.
  • Selection of eligible students for this scholarship is based on their previous academic performance and financial stability.
  • Each student's application will be verified by corresponding senior advisors and Head of the department. If any false information encountered, scholarship committee will have complete descretion to take any action.

List Of Mandatory Documents

  • X And XII Marks Sheet
  • Kerala Entrance Rank Certificate
  • Mark list of all semesters
  • Family Income Certificate Or Salary Slip (For 3 Months)
  • Tuition/Hostel/Mess Paid Receipts
  • Statement Of Estimated Expenses for the current acadmeic year
  • Bank Passbook Copy For Bank Account Details Confirmation
  • Receipt of any Bank loan if any
  • E-Aadhaar Or Scanned Copy Of Your Original Aadhaar
  • Receipt of any other Financial Assistance/Scholarship, if any.
